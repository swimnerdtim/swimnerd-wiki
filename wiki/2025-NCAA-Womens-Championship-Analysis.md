## Strategic Coaching Applications

### Relay Assembly Strategy

**For 200 Medley:**
1. **Backstroke:** Target sub-24 leadoff (Curzan standard: 23.17)
2. **Breaststroke:** Sub-26 split required for elite relays (Walsh: 25.62)
3. **Butterfly:** Sub-21 is championship standard (G. Walsh: 20.88)
4. **Freestyle anchor:** Pressure performer, sub-22 (Parker: 21.43)

**Reaction Time Standards:**
- Elite relays: 0.00-0.25s
- Virginia's relay: All under 0.23s
- **Train relay exchanges with timing systems**

### 800 Free Relay Benchmarks
