# ASU Butterfly Quality Set: 20x50s Progression

**Source:** Bob Bowman Arizona State 400 IM Group, Oct 19, 2022  
**Context:** Wednesday quality workout for 400 IM group, race-pace training  
**Athlete examples:** Molly, Chase, Reagan (ASU swimmers)

## The Workout Structure

### Preliminary Work (~3,500y)
- Extensive warm-up
- Underwater kicking
- Pulling set
- **Purpose:** Get most volume done before main set to minimize fatigue after quality work

### Main Set: 20x50 Fly Progression
**Format:** Build quality in waves
- 2 × 50 fly fast on :50
- 4 × 50 fly fast on :50  
- 6 × 50 fly fast on :50
- 8 × 50 fly fast on :50

**Recovery between rounds:** 2 × 50 free on :40 (six-beat kick, maintain speed)

**Work-to-rest ratio:** 1:1 (aiming for 50 seconds total, ~25-second swim)

### Performance Standards
**Elite times achieved:**
- **Men (Chase):** Consistent 23-lows with 4-stroke first lap, 5-stroke second lap
- **Women (Reagan):** Consistent 25-highs, couple 26-lows

**Technical requirements:**
- Consistent stroke count every 50
- Consistent kick count (DPS focus)
- Max VO2 butterfly pace
- More 200-fly oriented than 100-fly

## Coaching Principles

### Why Progress 2-4-6-8 Instead of 8-6-4-2?

**Early Season (Fall):** 2→4→6→8 progression
- Build up quality over time
- Focus on **speed endurance**
- First round = establish stroke/speed
- Each round = extend that quality
- Goal: Maintain, don't accelerate

**Late Season (Spring/Taper):** 8→6→4→2 descent
- Get faster each round
- Focus on **pure speed**
- Reverse fatigue to sharpen turnover

### Energy System Target
- **Max VO2** butterfly work
- 200-fly specific (not 100-fly sprinting)
- Building aerobic power at race pace
- Quality over volume after preliminary work

## Application for Masters/Age Group

**Adjust intervals:**
- Masters A: 50s on 1:00-1:10
- Masters B: 50s on 1:15-1:30
- Age group: Scale to swimmer ability (aim for 1:1.5 work:rest)

**Adjust progression:**
- Newer swimmers: 2-3-4-5 instead of 2-4-6-8
- Advanced: Keep full progression
- Always do even sets (2-4-6-8, not 3-5-7)

**Key coaching cues:**
- "Same stroke count every 50"
- "Hold the quality, don't accelerate"
- "This is your 200 fly race pace"
