## Historical Context

This workout exemplifies Urbanchek's October base-building philosophy:
1. **High volume** without excessive intensity
2. **Threshold work** emphasized early and often  
3. **Customized training** within team structure
4. **Systematic progression** (long to short, building effort)

By Day #169 (only 3 days into official practice), Michigan swimmers are already logging 8,000-yard threshold sessions. This volume and quality foundation would pay dividends at NCAAs 169 days later.
