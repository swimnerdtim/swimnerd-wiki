## 3. SIGNATURE SETS EVOLUTION

### Sets That Appeared in ALL Three Seasons

#### **A. "The IM 75's" (12x75 the IM 75's)**
- **Frequency:** Season 1: 5x | Season 2: 7x | Season 3: 8x
- **Evolution:** Originally 1:30 interval → evolved to 1:30-1:45 with complexity variations
- **Stroke Patterns:**
  - 25 Fly/Back/Breast (rotating combinations)
  - 50 combos (Fly/Bk, Bk/Brst, Brst/Free)
  - IM order variations

**Why It Works:** Technical rehearsal + aerobic conditioning + stroke balance in 900m. Perfect masters set.

#### **B. "The 75@400 Pace + 25 EZ" (Threshold Repeats)**
- **Frequency:** Season 1: 4x | Season 2: 9x | Season 3: 11x
- **Progression:** 10x→12x→14x→16x→18x reps across seasons
- **Intervals:** Stable at 1:50-2:00 (75-sec effort + rest)

**Why It's Genius:** Broken 100s disguised as 75s. Swimmers hit threshold without mental fatigue of reading "20x100."

#### **C. "The Grant Hackett Set" (40x50 Descending FAST)**
- **Appearances:** 3x across seasons (Opening workout signature)
- **Structure:**
  - 16x50 every 4th FAST
  - 12x50 every 3rd FAST
  - 8x50 every other FAST
  - 4x50 ALL FAST
- **Interval:** 1:00 throughout

**Why It's Repeated:** Season opener/tone-setter. Tests fitness, builds mental toughness, references elite training (Hackett credibility).

#### **D. "FKOB (Fins-Kick-On-Back) + Tarzan + Free" Progressions**
- **Frequency:** 38+ variations across all seasons
- **Typical Structure:** 
  - 200 FKOB / 4x50 Free (Season 1)
  - 8x50 O-FKOB, E-Tarzan (Season 2)
  - 8x75 25 FKOB/25 Tarzan/25 Free (Season 3)

**Evolution:** Started as straight sets → became drill progressions → integrated into warmups.

#### **E. "Broken 100s/200s at Pace"**
- **Season 1:** 4x{4x25 Broken 100 Pace}
- **Season 2:** 4x{50+100+50 Broken 200}
- **Season 3:** 16x25 100 Pace, 20x25 100 Pace

**Trend:** More granular breakdowns over time (100→50→25 splits), easier pacing feedback for masters.
