## 📏 200 Freestyle: Championship-Level Pacing

**Champion: Luke Hobson (Texas) - 1:28.33 (NCAA/American/US Open RECORD)**

### Hobson's Record-Breaking Splits

- **50:** 20.68 (reaction +0.74)
- **100:** 43.01 (22.33 split)
- **150:** 1:05.51 (22.50)
- **200:** 1:28.33 (22.82) ← **Closed strong**

### The Even-Split Standard

**Top 4 finishers split patterns:**

1. **Hobson (1:28.33):** 22.33 → 22.50 → 22.82 (slight build)
2. **Guiliano (1:29.42):** 22.63 → 23.06 → 23.44 (gradual fade)
3. **Jett (1:30.08):** 22.81 → 23.15 → 23.17 (died on finish)
4. **Hawke (1:30.14):** 22.77 → 23.20 → 23.27 (controlled fade)

### Winning Margin Analysis

**1st to 2nd:** 1.09 seconds (dominant)  
**2nd to 8th:** 2.03 seconds (tight field)

**Implication for coaches:**  
- **1-2 spots:** Need record-breaking swim  
- **2-8 spots:** Execution matters more than raw talent

### The Fatal Back-Half Collapse

**Crooks (6th, 1:31.36):**
- Out in 44.26 (23.46 second 50)
- Came home in 23.50, 23.60 ← **Slower than Hobson at record pace**

**The unforgiving math:** Can't win 200 free if your back 100 is slower than someone swimming 1:28

## 💡 Coaching Takeaways

### Sprint Training Principles

**1. Reaction Time Is Coachable**
- Elite range: +0.55 to +0.65
- **Train with electronic start systems** - Feedback accelerates learning
- **Video every start** - Visual confirmation of movement patterns

**2. Underwater Matters More Than You Think**
- 50 free winner (Crooks): Not mentioned, but likely elite breakout
- **Every 0.1 sec underwater = 0.3-0.4 sec final time improvement**

**3. Back-Half Speed Wins 100s and 200s**
- Liendo won 100 fly on back half (22.93 vs. Rose 23.39)
- Hobson set 200 free record with 22.82 final 50 (fastest in field)

### Race Strategy by Event

**50 Free:**
✅ Perfect reaction (+0.55 to +0.65)  
✅ Explosive underwater (0-15m determines race)  
✅ Full sprint from breakout - no "building speed"

**100 Fly:**
✅ Controlled first 50 (20.0-20.5)  
✅ Maximize turn speed (underwa

ters!)  
✅ Build last 50 - don't die (sub-23 required)

**200 Free:**
✅ Even pacing (±0.3 sec per 50)  
✅ Fastest 50: Usually #3 (around turn)  
✅ Close strong - last 50 separates medal from also-ran
