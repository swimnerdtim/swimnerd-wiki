## Coaching Notes

### Sprint Training Principles

**1. Full recovery between reps**
- Sprint = maximum effort requires maximum recovery
- 1:3 to 1:5 work:rest ratio for true sprints

**2. Quality > Quantity**
- 8 perfect 25s >> 16 mediocre 25s
- Maintain excellent technique even when fatigued

**3. Race-specific pacing**
- Practice target race pace + slightly faster
- Build database of what pace feels like

### Common Sprint Set Structures

**Short sprints (25s):**
- 8-16 x 25 @ :30-1:00
- Fins optional (adds power + reduces stroke count)

**Mid sprints (50s):**
- 6-12 x 50 @ :45-1:00
- Descending intervals build intensity

**Long sprints (75-100s):**
- 4-8 x 75-100 @ 1:15-2:00
- Best average or descending format

### Integration with Training Cycle

**Base phase:** 1 sprint session/week  
**Build phase:** 2 sprint sessions/week  
**Peak phase:** 2-3 sprint sessions/week  
**Taper:** Maintain sprint volume, reduce total volume
